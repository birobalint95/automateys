Our mission

At Automateys, our mission is to empower small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) by making process automation simple, accessible, and impactful. We recognize that many SMEs struggle with repetitive, time-consuming tasks that inflate operational costs and limit growth.

We are building an intuitive, SaaS-based platform that enables SMEs to automate these processes with ease, without requiring deep technical expertise. Our solution combines the power of advanced automation tools with a user-friendly, no-code interface tailored specifically to SME needs. By leveraging community-validated templates and an AI-powered process assistant, we reduce complexity and accelerate adoption, allowing businesses to increase operational capacity and improve efficiency. Our platform seamlessly integrates with existing business ecosystems, scales alongside company growth, and fosters collaboration within industry communities to continuously enhance automation value.

Through democratizing automation, we strive to help SMEs worldwide reduce costs, boost revenue, and adapt swiftly to evolving market demands‚Äîfueling sustainable economic growth and resilience.
